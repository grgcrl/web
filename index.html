GREGORY DE GIACOMI
office@gregorydegiacomi.com
Born in 1994, Gregory De Giacomi grew up in Vicenza, Italy. He studied architecture at the Accademia di architettura di Mendrisio receiving a master's degree in 2020.  From 2021 to 2026, he worked in the office of Valerio Olgiati, where he led the construction of the house in Avers at 2000 m above sea level.  In 2026, he founded his own architecture practice.
