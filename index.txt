GREGORY DE GIACOMI office@gregorydegiacomi.com Born in St.Moritz in 1994, Gregory De Giacomi grew up in Vicenza, Italy. He studied architecture at the Accademia di Architettura di Mendrisio receiving a master's degree in 2020. From 2021 to 2026, he has worked in the office of Valerio Olgiati, where he has led the realization of the house in Avers at 2000 m above sea level. In 2026, he founded his own architecture practice.
